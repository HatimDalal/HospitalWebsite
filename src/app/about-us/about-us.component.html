Rights of Patients

  • Right to respect for personal dignity and privacy during examination, procedures and treatment.
  • Right to protection from physical abuse or neglect.
  • Right to treating patient information as confidential.
  • Right to obtaining informed consent before carrying out procedures.
  • Right to information on how to voice a complaint.

Responsibility of Patients

  • To faithfully undergo the agreed therapy.
  • To follow the doctors instructions diligently.
  • To take necessary preventive measures in case of infectious disease as per the doctors instructions.
