About US

Welcome to Trinity Hospital

Founded in the year 1985, Trinity Hospital is one of the best multi-speciality hospitals in Delhi. Trinityhospital over the years has emerged as the prime provider of high quality professional medical care. Located in Safdarjung Enclave, Trinity Hospital is a state-of-the-art medical facility that is very well equipped with all modern facilities and specialised equipment. Its team of doctors is highly skilled and experienced. The nurses and other support staff at the hospital are professionally trained. For its patients, the name Trinity Hospital is synonymous with best patient care. The hospital is NABH accredited hospital and provides a wide spectrum of medical services as well as advanced surgical techniques. Other than being 28-bedded, the hospital also has pre and post operating rooms with gas lines, monitoring equipment, and defibrillator. The patients have the option to choose deluxe suites, premium rooms, twin sharing or triple sharing. Along with that, the hospital also offers Preventive Health Up Packages that can be customized as per the needs of corporates and outpatient consulting rooms. It also has a NABL accredited pathology lab and an imaging centre that complies to AERB.
